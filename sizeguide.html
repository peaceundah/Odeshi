Fit Guide

Odeshi garments are engineered for an oversized, boxy fit. We recommend ordering your true size for the intended silhouette, or sizing down for a more standard fit.

Measurement Method

Chest: Measured across the chest 1" below the armhole.
Length: Measured from the highest point of the shoulder to the bottom hem.
